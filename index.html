Eta Chapter pillars

Brotherhood, Scholarship, and Service form the foundation of our chapter. We honor those values through mentoring, awards, and community leadership.

Brotherhood Leadership and fellowship that connect members on campus and in the community.
Scholarship Academic development, tutoring, and programs that support student success.
Service Outreach events that create impact for Greensboro residents and A&T families.
Our chapter

Eta Chapter at NC A&T

Chartered in 1919, Eta Chapter continues a long lineage of Sigma brothers who prioritize service, academic achievement, and meaningful campus engagement. Our chapter remains active through mentorship, workshops, community events, and leadership development.

Focused on impact. We build programs that foster opportunity and celebrate the values of Phi Beta Sigma.
